A Couple Projects of Mine


Text-Based Dungeoneering Game: Krolo

While this project is a work-in-progress for one of my classes, I'm still proud of the work that I have been putting in and the work that I will continue to put into the creation of this text-based game. This game brings the player on an adventure through a labrynth, fighting various monsters such as goblins, spiders, trolls, skeletons, zombies, and a final boss, a lych. As the player progresses, they will earn treasure such as armor, weapons, and artifacts that will guide them on their journey as they get stronger and face harder and harder enemies.

The link for the source code will appear once the project is done




Form Registration and Confirmation

I wrote a short program using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript combined to take user-input through a form, validates the input, and converts it into a confirmation page, neatly and cleanly organized for display. While this form is short in length, it demonstrates the use of different input fields such as text, email, telephone numbers, radio buttons, and checkboxes to showcase the abilities of web-design structures.
